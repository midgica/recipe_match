Moo Shu Pork Tacos

  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 c green onion
  • 1/2 head red cabbage
  • 8 oz mushrooms
  • 3 radishes
  • 1 tbsp ginger
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1/3 c hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • tortillas or other flatbread
  • sriracha
  • 1/4 c mayonnaise
  1. Fry mushrooms and half the green onion in sesame oil.
  2. Mix the hoisin sauce, vinegar, and soy sauce.
  3. Mix the mayo with enough sriracha to make a peach color, or to taste.
  4. Add pork, ginger, and garlic to the hot pan.
  5. Once the pork is cooked, add the hoisin mixture and the cabbage. Cook until cabbage reaches desired tenderness.
  6. Serve on warmed tortillas or flatbread, topped with raw green onion, radish slices, and sriracha mayo.
